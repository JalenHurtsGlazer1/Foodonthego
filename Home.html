Food On The Go

About Us

Our goal is to inform people, both domestic and foreign, about which foods are popular. We don’t just do one type of food, yet multiple. We prefer a wide variety from BBQ to Street Food. It’s important for people to be educated on the many different types of food.

Social Media Reviews

Each food example will have a review by a famous athlete, specifically a football player. These reviews are to show the users of our websites that there are many other people with opinions, but also try and draw them into our website. Through research, we found that people are more likely to listen to a famous person, so we included their thoughts. Some of the athletes that will appear include Drake Maye and Dan Campbell.
